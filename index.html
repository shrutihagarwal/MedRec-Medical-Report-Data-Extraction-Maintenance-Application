About Us

MedRec is an online record-keeping system with the aim to store each and every medical examination report that a patient receives, Keeping track of it all can be a challenge. With a personal health record, you can gather and manage all that information in one easily accessible location. which can be made simpler to obtain in an emergency. Medrec offers a complete solution for storing, accessing, and retrieving medical reports.
